Public Relations Department for Arab and Foreign Patients

The public relations department for Arab and foreign patientshas well experienced staff that aim to facilitate your medical journey all the way from your country to The Specialty Hospitalto be treated by the best doctors andcovering allspecialties.

The department offers:

When the patient sends the medical report the department connects the patient with the needed specialist to give a free consultation prior to the patient's travel in which the specialist will give an initial diagnosis and the needed treatment plan with the length of stay.

Facilitate visa issuing and travel arrangements.

Arrange airport pick up and transfer for the patient and the companions to the hotel or hospital.

An ambulancecar can be provided for emergency cases from the airport or country borders to arrange transportation to The Specialty Hospital.

For your comfort,accommodation can be arranged for you and your companions near the Specialty Hospital as per your request (hotel or apartment).

Arrange your appointment with your specialist.

Arrange for touristic sightseeing of Jordan upon request for the patient and the companions according to the patient's medicalstatus.

Follows up the treatment journey from admission, treatment anduntil the patient is discharged. And also follow after they are back home.
