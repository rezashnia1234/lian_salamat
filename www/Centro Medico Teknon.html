Centro Médico Teknon

Teknon Medical Center is one of the leading hospitals in the private health sector in Spain, with its high quality of medical and welfare service in an environment that combines personal attention with the support of the latest technology. Teknon Medical Center's mission is to offer the best medical and ethical treatment to patients treated at our facilities achieving excellence in service care and humane treatment.

Teknon is governed by a rigorous culture of quality management, focused on providing the best care and patient satisfaction with the maximum security badge that has earned it the prestigious Joint Commission International accreditation of centers and The Generalitat of Catalonia (based on the model of excellence EFQM European Foundation for Quality Management), becoming one of the few Spanish centers that has both distinctions.

Centro Médico Teknon is a proud partner and subsidiary of Quirón Salud.

A medical team of highest excellence

In our campus exert more than 400 front-line specialists with international training in each of their areas and patient care in several languages. A total of more than 2,000 professionals oversee the care, comfort and personalized attention for patients who rely on us.

The optional body Teknon covers all medical and surgical specialties, being an international leader in Oncology, Cardiology and Cardiovascular Surgery, Traumatology, Maxillofacial Surgery, Cosmetic Surgery and Neurosurgery. In this area, the center is also known for advising second opinions.

Comprehensive health service

Teknon was opened in 1994 and covers more than 60,000 square meters of land. The campus is divided into eight main areas, connected and with direct access from the 1,000 parking spaces at its center. The center has 211 single rooms, 19 suites.

Neonatal unit where the babies who have problems at birth or during the first weeks of life are met. Neonatal Unit provides the highest level of care excellence and includes those processes that require neonatal surgery. The area is equipped with 10 incubators designed to neonatal intensive care and intermediate care, and five cribs for infants requiring observation.

The Day Hospital was created for those patients who need to be under observation for a few hours in the type of intervention or process. We currently have two Day Hospitals boxes 17 and 14, respectively, for those medical-surgical procedures performed at our center.

The Intensive Care Unit - Coronary Unit has 14 boxes of UCI-UC multipurpose for children and adults.

The Emergency Department is permanently on call 24 hours a day, 365 days a year. It has 21 boxes and 6 medical teams on duty serving more than 270 daily emergency. The Surgical Area has 20 operating rooms equipped with the latest technology, where more than 22,000 operations are performed annually. The area Diagnostics Service in which more than 145,000 outpatient diagnostic tests are performed annually.

The Teknon Oncology Institute (IOT) is a leader in the prognosis, diagnosis and treatment of cancer and is organized on the model of Memorial Sloan Kettering Cancer Center in New York. The Cardiovascular Teknon (ICVT) Institute is dedicated to comprehensively study, diagnosis and complete treatment of cardiovascular diseases.

Offices with a medical staff of more than 400 medical specialists covering all medical-surgical adult and pediatric specialties. Offices area has 2 buildings with 150 medical consultation offices, with direct connection to the clinic and direct access to parking.
