Heidelberg University Hospital

Medicine of the Highest Standard

Year established: 1386

Heidelberg University Hospital is one of Europe`s leading medical centers. World-renowned experts provide comprehensive care of the highest international standards in all medical specialties. Heidelberg University Hospital attracts patients from all over Germany and many other countries.

Scientific Excellence for Your Health - for more than 600 Years

The history of medicine in Heidelberg dates back to the year 1386. The defining attribute of Heidelberg University is its relentless aspiration to higher academic performance together with the recognition of its hospital as an international hallmark for quality. In 2007, Heidelberg University was appointed as a German Elite University by the German Research Association and the German Council of Science and Humanities.
