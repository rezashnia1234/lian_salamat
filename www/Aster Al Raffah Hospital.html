Aster Al Raffah Hospital

Aster Al Raffah Hospitals & Clinics Oman is proud to be one of the largest hospital networks in the region, which initially started with a Medical Centre in Sohar, has now to its credit, 2 full-fledged state-of-the-art hospitals, 6 Polyclinics with few other ongoing projects. Aster Al Raffah Hospital Ghubra, started as a 30 bedded hospital which is now converted into a 64 bedded hospital, due to the immense acceptance by the public of Oman.

Aster Al Raffah Hospital offers a full range of medical services from General Medicine, General Surgery, ENT, Paediatrics & Neonatology, Dermatology, Ophthalmology, Dentistry, Gynecology & Obstetrics to super specialty services like, Cardiology, Urology, Neurology, Plastic surgery, Gastroenterology, Nephrology, Psychiatry and Laparoscopic Surgery.

It has to its credit some of the most leading surgeons & physicians in the world. With a full fledged round the clock OT facility, ICU & 8 bedded emergency, Aster Al Raffah Hospital is equipped with most advanced equipment & expertise to provide world class care 24 x 7.
