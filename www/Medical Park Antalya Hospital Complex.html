Medical Park Antalya Hospital

The Mediterranean region's largest private hospital of the Medical Park Antalya Hospital Complex with 84 outpatient rooms, 39 of a total of 228 beds, including the Heart Hospital, 73 ICU / 10 coronary care / 7 KVC intensive care / 22 general ICU / 34 neonatal intensive care units and transplant surgery, with 10 operating rooms equipped with the standard 33 serving the medical industry.

Medical Park Antalya Healthcare Complex offers services and treatments with international standards. It is located at Lara Beach which is one of the most important business and living locations of Antalya and offer services with "Hospital Complex" concept consisting of combination of Tertiary Hospital and Heart Hospital at a closed area over 30,000 square meters.

Internationally recognized Medical Park Antalya Hospital Complex has corporate agreements with more than 200 domestic and foreign insurance companies.

Medical Park Antalya is operated by the Medical Park Hospitals Group, the biggest hospital group in Turkey.

Cost Estimates and Billing Inquiries

International patient referrals are examined and assessed by a specialized medical staff so that cost estimates for treatment can be provided to patients before they travel to Medical Park. In order to improve the accuracy of quotes, patients will be asked to provide their most recent medical records or diagnosis in English.

The clinical coordinator will provide estimates and deposit information to ensure his or her understanding of charges and costs.

 

Appointments

Medical Park International Patient Center will refer each inquiry to the appropriate department and schedule the earliest appointment convenient for both patient and physician.

 

Visas and Passport Information

Medical Park will support your medical visa application by working with Turkish embassies or consulates in your country to provide documentation of your pending medical treatment and appointment. The Center will make every effort to expedite the processing of your visa.

 

Travel and Transportation

The staff can advise you of the most convenient connections to reach Turkey, where a Medical Park representative will meet you and your accompanying family members upon your arrival. This services is free to patients and accompanying family members.

 

Accommodations

Medical Park International Patient Center maintains relationships with a number of hotels and residential businesses. After you arrive, Medical Park representative will work with you to identify accommodations that best suit your needs.
