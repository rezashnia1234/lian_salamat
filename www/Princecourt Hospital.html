It gives me great pleasure to welcome you to Prince Court Medical Centre – a hospital  built with a vision, mission and values to provide one of the highest quality health care while committed to achieving and maintaining a standard of excellence in all we do.

At Prince Court, patient comfort and satisfaction is key!

While we take into account sensitivity to patient’s needs, we’re committed to provide high-quality medical care in a friendly environment. Our medical staff includes skilled and experienced physicians representing a wide range of medical specialties, well trained and experienced nurses, allied health personnel, medical technicians and supporting staff who are dedicated and committed to providing  you our best service and care while you are with us.

We not only treat, care and manage our patients but also embark on disease prevention activities and preventive health services to support the Government  in its move to have a healthy nation. We encourage Malaysians of all walks of life to take control of their health and manage it through our executive health screening programs.  Our website,  www.princecourt.com is designed to provide useful information about us and the comprehensive healthcare services and facilities we offer.

At Prince Court , we want your experience to be as pleasant, convenient and comfortable as possible. Most importantly, we’re here to help you get well, get healthy, and get your life back to normal as quickly as possible.
