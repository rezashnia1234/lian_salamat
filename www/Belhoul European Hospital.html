Belhoul European Hospital

Belhoul European Hospital was established in 1996 as the first day care hospital in the UAE. It currently provides comprehensive medical facilities through its 22 outpatient clinics. The medical specialities are supported by diagnostic facilities including radiology and laboratory. Other specialized support services include speech therapy, laser therapy, and physiotherapy which support multi-speciality clinics at the hospital.

The hospital has become an effective provider of medical health in the region, and is committed to the health of the community by constantly striving to improve on the standard of medical services offered.

The vision is to create a unique health care model for the UAE by providing comprehensive medical services with innovative health care solutions.
