Ganzouri Specialized Hospital

Ganzouri Specialized Hospital is a private general hospital that has been in business for more than 40 years .   It was founded by Dr. Mostafa El-Ganzouri in the Year 1971. It is one of the first private hospitals built at that time , located right in the heart of Cairo with 100 beds. The hospital campus is divided into four inter-connected buildings with a total of 11,000 sq meters of buildings, built on 3275 sq. meters of land in the center of the city.

We serve a large mix of patients mainly from Cairo but some from the Arab world as well and Nigeria.

We are growing to be multi-chain organization and we have policlinic to have different geographic location access , and we will be opening a new extension to increase our capacity to 190 beds within 3 month .

We have opened a tele-medicine unit and we provide a second medical opinion either on line or off line through a network of center of excellence.  We seek to contract with a group of center of excellence to provide world class service multi-specialty and in case the patient needs to travel abroad we provide travel and lodging services for our patients and follow up.
