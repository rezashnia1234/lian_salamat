Fortis Hospital Shalimar Bagh

The Fortis Shalimar Bagh hospital is located in North West Delhi. The Shalimar Bagh area is well known for it's beautiful gardens, making it a pleasant choice for convalescent patients. It is also conveniently located to amenities, with the new shopping mall just 5 mins away.

The Fortis Shalimar Bagh Hospital was established in 2010 as a private hospital. And has expended from a 250 bed facility to 550 beds. The highly talented surgeons and medical staff are qualified to provide top class treatment in all specialities. And they are dedicated to giving all patients friendly and compassionate care. Patients are also offered wellness and preventive health.

The Fortis Shalimar Bagh is the first hospital in India to be registered for the 'green building rating certification'. The building has been designed as an energy efficient structure that complies with the Energy Conservation Building Code (ECBC)

Only the latest in technology and diagnostic equipment are used, including Cardiac Volume from Siemens with adaptive spiral 4D facility, Endoscopy Suites and Flat Panel Cath Lab.

The hospital also offers valuable assistance to their international patients, such as visa and travel assistance, insurance, airport transfer service, language interpreters and local sight-seeing trips.
